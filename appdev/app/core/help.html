Practical Startpage
Practical Startpage is designed to fit links and other functionality best possible on a single page.
Responsive design
To accomplish this the design is built responsive, i.e. the content adjust to give best fit for different screen sizes. The columns and content adjusts to give best viewing experience for all screen widths.
Content
The page consists of three columns, but for smaller screens the right column merges with the center column to allow the content to be usable.
In the case of the link lists content is spread over two columns, which collapse to one for small screens.
